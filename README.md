# Odoo 19 ERP Implementation Case Study



Simulated end-to-end Odoo 19 ERP implementation for a medical supplies trading company.



## Modules

- Inventory

- Purchase

- Sales

- Accounting



## Functional Areas

- Business Analysis

- Inventory Reconciliation

- Inventory Valuation (FIFO & AVCO)

- Three-Way Matching

- Approval Workflows

- Putaway Rules

- Landed Cost Management

- Replenishment Planning

- UAT & Go-Live



## Key Results

- Identified and resolved 2,835 JOD in financial discrepancies

- Corrected a 910 JOD inventory valuation gap

- Prevented 1,925 JOD in AP discrepancies through procurement controls

- Implemented inventory traceability and warehouse controls

- Improved profitability through sourcing and landed cost analysis
